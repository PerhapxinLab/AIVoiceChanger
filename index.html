AI Voice Transform

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

✓ Zero Data Collection

Short version: This app runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No servers are contacted. Your voice never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. AI Voice Transform does not collect any personal data, usage statistics, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

How your voice data is handled

All voice processing happens locally on your device using the built-in AI model. Specifically:

  • Voice files you import are processed on-device and never uploaded anywhere
  • Recordings made within the app stay on your device
  • Converted audio is saved locally to your device only
  • The app does not require an internet connection to function

Third-party services

None. This app does not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or third-party services that could access your data.

Microphone permission

The app requests microphone access only to let you record your voice directly within the app. This audio is processed locally and is never transmitted.

Data storage

Files you create (converted audio, voice library entries) are stored in your device's local storage only. You can delete them at any time by removing them from the app or via the Files app.

Children's privacy

This app does not collect data from any users, including children under 13. It is rated 4+ on the App Store.
